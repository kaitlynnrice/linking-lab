The time I... was stranded

By: Kaitlyn Rice

The flight was long and uneventful, but once we got off the plane we immediately got our car and drove to the blue lagoon.

During our roadtrips around parts of the island, we picked up many hitchhikers along the way:

  • 2 guys from America (Colorado and Vermont)
  • A couple from Ukraine
  • Group from the Czech Republic working there over the summer

On our last full day, we did an ATV excursion around the countryside where we visited gysters and learned more about their geothermal power production.

Sadly, it came time for us to leave the beautiful island of Iceland.
