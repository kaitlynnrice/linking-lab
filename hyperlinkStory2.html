Not having a lot of time to prepare, I immediately start getting my shifts at work covered, pulled out my winter clothes, and started packing. The weirdest part was I was packing for 2 different climates. Summer in Iceland is still very cold, but in Sweden it was the opposite.

Our itinerary included:
